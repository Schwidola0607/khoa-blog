Sequence Parallelism for Inference: Part 1

Introduction

Nd+ parallelism techniques are often invented for training first, then adapted for inference. Most techniques have seen great success in today’s inference world (both individually and together). However, Sequence Parallelism has not seen wide adoption in the open source world. If you look at vLLM or SGLang today, sequence parallelism is either in not-so-active development or completely deprioritized.
